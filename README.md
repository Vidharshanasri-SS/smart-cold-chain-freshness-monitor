The Smart Cold Chain Freshness Monitor is an innovative IoT-based solution designed to ensure the safety, quality, and freshness of perishable products during storage and transportation. The system continuously monitors critical environmental parameters such as temperature, humidity, air quality, and volatile organic compounds (VOC) in real time. By integrating sensors like the DHT11/DHT22, MQ135, and SGP40 with an ESP32 microcontroller, the system provides continuous environmental surveillance throughout the cold chain process.

As the project grows, future enhancements will include AI-based freshness prediction, predictive analytics, automated alert systems, GPS-enabled shipment tracking, mobile application support, and advanced cloud-based monitoring services. The ultimate goal is to build an intelligent and data-driven cold chain ecosystem that minimizes losses, improves sustainability, and ensures the delivery of fresh and safe products to consumers.

This project represents a step toward creating a smarter, more efficient, and technologically advanced cold chain infrastructure, with the long-term ambition of developing it into a successful technology startup that delivers real-world impact.
